Aquatic Intimacies poster series

Vertical posters: 33 × 47 in

Horizontal posters: 22 × 17 in

2021

Continuing my exploration of interspecies intimacy, eroticism, desire, embodiment, queerness, and transhumanism, I designed a poster series that probed the method of collage as a visual metaphor for intimacy — the coming together of bodies — the placing of the content of one thing into the form of another — the coalescence of the forms of multiple things into each other — the proximity of disparate content.
