Precarious Pics website

html, css, javascript

2022

I designed a site to host photos of marine creatures taken by a friend in Palau. The photos swim through the site like fish and run away from the cursor when you try to click them to view the full image. I overlaid the photos with text detailing current political developments in Palau that threaten the future of the marine sanctuary, as well as a petition for users to sign.
