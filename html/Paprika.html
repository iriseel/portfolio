Paprika broadsheet

25 × 22.75 in, double-sided sheet
2023

I co-designed the “Fables” issue of Paprika, a journal published by the Yale architecture school, with Darnell Henderson. Given the theme, our design referenced the oral storytelling traditions of fables and the way in which fantastical stories like fairytales are often accompanied by elaborate visual frames.
