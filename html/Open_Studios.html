Open Studios identity, signage

Clear and fluorescent acrylic
2023

In the spring of 2023, the Yale School of Art hosted its annual Open Studios, drawing a record-setting 1100 attendees. Jisung Park (Yale ’23) and I designed the identity and fabricated cutout acrylic wayfinding signs for all 4 graduate departments, the undergraduate studios, and 2 new satellite galleries.

We were inspired by shape rulers and the idea of creating signage from both the positive and negative “space” of our signage materials. Jisung designed a custom typeface for the identity.

Major features of the project include: Lasercut signage; custom typeface.
