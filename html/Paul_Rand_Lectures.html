Paul Rand Lectures poster series

moving and print posters
2023

In the fall of 2023, I designed the posters for the Yale School of Art Graphic Design department's Paul Rand Lecture series. I built a site that tracked my face via the webcam, and then used that data to make moving posters. The videos were played across 3 monitors near the building entrance, with the volume of each poster calibrated to the lecture date: the poster(s) for upcoming lectures were loud, while posters for past lectures were tuned down.
